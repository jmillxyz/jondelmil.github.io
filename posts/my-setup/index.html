My Setup

I’m always interested in the tools that experts in a given domain use day-to-day. I am by no means an expert, but would love to learn about new tools that I haven’t tried.

Sidenote: I work from home.

If you’ve got a good suggestion, please reach out!

Mobile mode

Coffee shops, when I go down to the office, etc

Apple MacBook Pro (Retina, 15”, mid-2015)

  • 16 GB RAM
  • Core i7 @ 2.5 GHz
  • 256 GB SSD
  • integrated GPU

The Last Good MacBook Pro. In all seriousness, I do prefer the feel of this keyboard to the newer ones. It helped my decision that it was issued as a term of my employment ;)

Apple Airpods

What a surprisingly great and versatile product. I use these not only for my runs and workouts, but almost always when I’m listening to music of any kind while on the go. Also great for video conferencing when I’m in public.

3M Peltor X4A earmuffs

Sometimes I want complete silence, instead of music. These are cheap and effective. Best of all, I can even slip the airpods in my ear and these over top!

Home mode

Dell 3415w ultrawide monitor

I can comfortably hold 3-4 full-screen windows open at once, and the resolution is almost exactly half that (dpi) of the retina MBP, so everything stays the same size as I drag windows across my screens.

Rain laptop stand

Looks great with matching light aluminum, and it happens to hold the bottom of the MBP screen at the same level as my Dell ultrawide.

cheap mechanical 98-key keyboard

I believe this has the cherry red equivalent keycaps. I’m thinking about getting a clicky keyboard that’s also silent, to help with video conferencing. Cherry clears, I think?

Logitech G500s mouse

Sometimes the button clicks won’t register perfectly, but I’ve gotten so used to the buttons on this that it’d probably be hard to go back to something else. I think what would probably push me over the edge is getting a completely wireless mouse.

Sennheiser HD600

I used to use the $300 Sennheiser HD600, but the Airpods’ lack of a cord has spoiled me. I break out the Sennheisers when I’m feeling like I want to hear every bit of the music I’m listening to, but that doesn’t actually happen very often.
